Final Girl

Summery

A 5 player game straight out of a 80s slasher film were 4 players play as victims who are trapped in a murder circus. They must use their wits and special skills to escape while another player who plays as one of the murderous circus preformers hunting them down. The victims will have to collect items while suriving a hand full of traps that are littered all around the circus grounds.
