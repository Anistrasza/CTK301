Aura

Summery

An strategy based free-for-all deathmatch card game where players play as witches trying to summon a demon. Players must search around a abandon house with the rumor of containing ingredients for a demon summoning ritual. Players must collect these items but they will have to battle other witches by using spells to steal and gather different ingredients to win.

Role on the Team

In the group, I had the role of Game Director, Game Designer, and Character Artist. I ended up taking up a leader like role in the group as I was the one who originally came up with the initial idea. I help decided on the main mechanics of the game as well as creating the story prompt for the game. I was also in charge of creating the character concept art.
